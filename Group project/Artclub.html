Art Club

Background Information

Sherubtse Art Club was re-established in 2016 by Chimi Wangchuk and Sonam Phuentsho to guide and assist the students of Sherubtse College to explore, expose and encourage them into art. Sherubtse Art Club focuses on improving the art skills and creativity of the members and out-campus students from the community. With this goal, the unit organizes a variety of activities including art classes for members and students, art competitions, and workshops. The major event organized by the Sherubtse Art Club is the art exhibition which is held annually at the art gallery, SSV. Today, the unit consists of about 70 members including 4 executives.

Mission

  • Teach, learn and promote art skills and education.
  • Guide and assist the students of Sherubtse College and youths of Kanglung to explore, expose and encourage them into art.
  • Contributing to the college and the community through socially beneficial activities.

Vision

To be a vibrant creative hub that nurtures artistic talent and fosters a culture of expression, appreciation, and innovation across Sherubtse College and the wider Kanglung community.
