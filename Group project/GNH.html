GNH Club

Background Information

Sherubtse GNH Youth Club would function under the auspices of the GNH Centre Bhutan, a non-profit civil society organization under the president of Her Royal Highness Ashi Kezang Choden Wangchuck. Sherubtse GNH Youth Club would support the GNHCB-led activities in both national and international programs.

Mission

  • To inspire youths through platforms in leadership, entrepreneurship, and social skills in promoting the values of GNH
  • To support the GNHCB-led activities in national and international forums.
  • To promote the club within the local community and wider campus practically.

Vision

Aspire to empower youths through values of GNH towards a happy society
